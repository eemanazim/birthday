A Little About You

She is the sun that lights my darkest skies,
The steady hand when the world feels unkind.
More than laughter, more than time,
She is the heartbeat that’s always mine.

She is my secret keeper, my endless cheer,
A whisper of comfort when I’m lost in fear.
More than everything, more than I can say,
She is my soul’s home, my bright every day.
